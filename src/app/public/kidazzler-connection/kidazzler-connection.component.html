Kidazzler Thinks Globally and Together We Act Locally!

Your business has the potential to enrich the lives of many families in your community. But, the sad reality for many families is that a lack of money prevents them from ever visiting, experiencing, and shopping with your business.

Kidazzler is helping change that by bringing families and businesses together to help everyone thrive. We believe that money should not be an obstacle when it comes to the well being of any child.

From day one, we built Kidazzler in a way that integrates giving back into our DNA.

We pledged to give a portion of our revenues back to our community.

Social Responsibility: Bringing our pledge full circle.

Through our pledge to give back, everyone benefits.

Kidazzler's member businesses will benefit from affordable all-in-one effective and targeted advertising. 

At the same time, families in the communities you serve will have extra money to spend on products and services at your business and other businesses in your community.

If it sounds like a beneficial circle, it is. And Kidazzler's pledge is the glue that binds it.
